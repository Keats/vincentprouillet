For profit

Proppy (2016 - now)

Proppy is a tool to make the whole proposals process less painful. No more back and forth via emails and simple looking proposals. Proppy gets everyone great looking proposals out of the box with e-signatures and analytics so you spend less time writing proposals and more time doing work.
Proppy has been featured on ProductHunt and on various magazines and newsletters.

Hizard (2014)

Hizard was a webapp to facilitate hiring at small companies by making it collaborative. It offered a job portal and a way to track applicants, their current status and what your colleagues thought of each applicant.
It was at the MVP phase (code available on github) and we were demo-ing to a few companies but ultimately stopped working on it for lack of time.
